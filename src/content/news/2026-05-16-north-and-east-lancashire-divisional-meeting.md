Representatives from the Division of Northumberland attended the 2026 Annual Divisional Meeting of the Division of North and East Lancashire on Saturday, 16 May 2026.

The meeting was held at Preston Masonic Hall, Saul Street, Preston, at the invitation of Right Illustrious Knight Stephen William Bolton, Intendant-General for North and East Lancashire. The formal Annual Divisional Meeting began at 11.00am, followed by luncheon at 1.15pm. 

Representing the Division of Northumberland were Intendant-General Trevor Fish, Deputy Intendant-General John Tomlin, and Divisional Recorder Paul McKay. Their attendance reflected the close and friendly links between neighbouring Divisions within the Masonic and Military Order of the Red Cross of Constantine and the Orders of the Holy Sepulchre and of St John the Evangelist.

The Northumberland delegation was warmly received and enjoyed the opportunity to share fellowship with members and guests from across North and East Lancashire. The occasion provided a welcome opportunity to support the Division’s Annual Meeting, renew friendships, and strengthen the bonds between the two Divisions.

The Division of Northumberland extends its thanks to Right Illustrious Knight Stephen William Bolton and the members of North and East Lancashire for their kind hospitality and a most enjoyable day.
