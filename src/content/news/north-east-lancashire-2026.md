The Division of Northumberland was warmly received at the Annual Divisional Meeting of the Division of North and East Lancashire, held on Saturday 16th May 2026 at the Masonic Hall, Saul Street, Preston.

Representing Northumberland were the Intendant-General, Right Illustrious Knight Trevor Fish, the Deputy Intendant-General, Illustrious Knight John Tomlin, and the Divisional Recorder, Knight Paul McKay. They were among the guests of the host Intendant-General, Right Illustrious Knight Stephen William Bolton, who presided over proceedings.

Following tea and coffee from 9.30 a.m., the Annual Meeting opened at 11.00 a.m. and was conducted with the dignity and fellowship for which the Order is known. Business concluded, the companions and their guests adjourned to luncheon at 1.15 p.m., where the conversation turned, as ever, to the strength of the bonds between neighbouring Divisions.

The Intendant-General extended the thanks of the Division of Northumberland to Right Illustrious Knight Bolton, to the Divisional Recorder Illustrious Knight Craig D Wood, and to all the companions of North and East Lancashire for their hospitality and the kindness shown to our visiting officers.
