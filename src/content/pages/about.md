The Northumberland Division comprises the Conclaves of the Order of the Red Cross of Constantine and its appendant Orders — the Holy Sepulchre and St John the Evangelist — meeting throughout Northumberland.

## Our purpose

The Order is a Christian Masonic body that draws its inspiration from the conversion of the Emperor Constantine and the early Christian Church. It complements other Masonic Orders by enriching the Craft journey with a distinctly devotional, fraternal and charitable focus.

## Membership

Membership of a Conclave is by invitation. Candidates must be subscribing Royal Arch Masons who profess the Trinitarian Christian faith. If you would like to know more about joining, please [contact the Divisional Recorder](/contact/).

## Governance

The Division is constituted under the Grand Imperial Conclave of England and Wales and its Districts and Conclaves Overseas, and is led by the **Intendant‑General for the Division of Northumberland**.
